Theme

Why Not? - The Case for Unimpeded Creativity, is a theme built of the absurdity of life and the tenacity of the creative spirit.

Everyone always asks “why?” They never ask “Why not?”

Asking “Why?” is always a good thing; it’s how we have made such leaps and bounds in research, science, medicine, and technology. But the best ideas have always come from asking “Why Not?” by challenging the status quo, each other, and even ourselves, to do better, reach farther, and open our minds to new ideas and possibilities.

Speaker Packet

The speaker packet is a guideline from TEDx and TEDxPortland for the speakers that are asked to make a talk that inspires and is correlated with the associated theme.

The guidelines help the speaker how to operate on stage and train them to make a “talk” and not a speach.
