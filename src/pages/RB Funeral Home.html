Ralston & Bredickas Funeral Home

Their website was originally plagued with two different design directions in one. Two different earth tone style were built together a content layout that was hard for clients to use.

A layout template was used as a base to get content direction going. Using imagery and common colors for the area in the fall season, a new thematic color style was made to clean up the site content hierarchy.
